The Uniqueness of Arlecchino: The Mystery of 'The Knave' Figure in Genshin Impact's Storyline

Arlecchino, also known as "The Knave", has become a major focus in the Genshin Impact storyline, especially after her appearance in "Teaser Interlude: Theatricality on a Winter's Night".

Despite being one of the Eleven Fatui Harbingers, her exact rank in the organization's hierarchy remains a mystery. However, through confirmation in Genshin Impact's live stream versions 4.0 and 4.1, Arlecchino's role as the main antagonist in Fontaine's storyline has been revealed. She owns an orphanage called "House of the Hearth", where she teaches children to become members of Fatui.

Her mysterious appearance, with pale skin, pure black iris eyes and red "x" shaped pupils, and long white hair with black streaks, adds to the uniqueness of her character. The name "Arlecchino" itself is taken from an early Italian theater name, and her presence in Fontaine will take center stage in the latest Archon Quest. As such, Arlecchino is an interesting character to follow in the development of Genshin Impact's storyline.
