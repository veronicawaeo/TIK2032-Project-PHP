QUADLIPS Embark on a Musical Journey with New Single 'Catch Me Kiss Me'

QUADLIPS, an international unit consisting of four members from group 48, namely Feni JKT48, Cole MNL48, Fame BNK48, and Hina SKE48. They will be active in their respective home countries as well as holding activities in various cities in Southeast Asia. QUADLIPS' official debut with the single "Catch Me Kiss Me" was marked by their first performance held in Bangkok. QUADLIPS' debut single "Catch Me Kiss Me" was released on digital platforms on March 11, 2024. On the same day, QUADLIPS also performed their debut single at Zone Beacon 3 centralwOrld Bangkok. All QUADLIPS members looked stunning with outfits that harmonized with the concept of their single.

JKT48 generation 3 member, Feni, was chosen as the member who will debut in this group. She is the oldest member who is also the leader of QUADLIPS. The single Catch Me Kiss Me has a hip-hop genre and emphasizes the rap skills of each member. The swag outfits and stage performance of the four members really made fans mesmerized.
